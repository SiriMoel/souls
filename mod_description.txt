This mod adds the ability to collect and use the souls of enemies for your spells.
Spells added by this mod have different effects depending on what type of soul you use to cast them.

[img]https://i.imgur.com/z1SIIJF.png[/img]

[b]35[/b] new spells
[b]2[/b] new perks
[b]5+[/b] new wands and items
[b]2[/b] new enemies
[b]1[/b] new boss
[b]3[/b] new structures
[b]3+[/b] new materials
[b]13[/b] soul types
Upgrade a magic tome with souls
Revive bosses
Create a Phylactery to enhance your survivability
Acquire unlimited souls through a new quest line

Kill enemies to collect souls. Different enemies drop different souls.
Use souls with spells added by this mod.

[img]https://i.imgur.com/EfsTYyC.png[/img]
