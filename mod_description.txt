A continuation & remaster of Tales of Kupoli's souls mechanic.

This mod adds the ability to collect and use the souls of enemies for your spells.
Spells added by this mod have different effects depending on what type of soul you use to cast them.

[img]https://i.imgur.com/z1SIIJF.png[/img]

[b]28[/b] new spells
[b]3+[/b] wands and items
[b]2[/b] new enemies
[b]2+[/b] new materials
[b]13[/b] soul types
An upgradeable magic tome
Revive bosses
Create a Phylactery to enhance your survivability

Kill enemies to collect souls, and cast spells with those souls.

[img]https://i.imgur.com/EfsTYyC.png[/img]

Cross-mod interactions with:
[b]Apotheosis[/b] 2 new soul types
This mod should be compatible with all content mods, except Tales of Kupoli.
